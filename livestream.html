About Our Game Jam

Join our vibrant community of game developers and enthusiasts in our monthly game jam! Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, our events offer a fantastic opportunity to unleash your creativity, collaborate with others, and have fun.

Our game jams are held once a month and include:

  • Exciting themes to inspire your game creation
  • Opportunity to showcase your games
  • Valuable feedback from experienced judges
  • Promotion for your game on our platforms
  • Networking opportunities with fellow developers

Every other month, we host a paid event with a chance to win a $1500 cash prize! The paid event includes:

  • Exclusive access to in-person workshops and speakers
  • Delicious meal included with your registration
  • Chance to compete for the cash prize

Can't make it to the paid event? Don't worry! Our free events are live-streamed, so you can still participate and showcase your creations to a global audience.
